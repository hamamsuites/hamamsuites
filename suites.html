The Suites

The establishment maintains 6 fully renovated suites.

Each suite is unique and includes private wellness facilities,

with some of them having a private hammam, while others jacuzzi or a sauna.

Most of them have a kitchenette and a terrace/patio.

You are welcome to roam through them and choose which suits you best.
